Key Anime Genres

Anime spans every genre imaginable — from action-packed adventures to quiet slice-of-life stories.

Action / Adventure

Shonen

Friendship, perseverance, and epic battles. The most popular genre globally.

Naruto, One Piece, Demon Slayer

Romance / Drama

Shojo

Emotional stories about relationships, personal growth, and self-discovery.

Sailor Moon, Fruits Basket

Mature

Seinen

Complex themes, psychological depth, and realistic storytelling for adults.

Berserk, Ghost in the Shell

Fantasy / Escapism

Isekai

Characters transported to another world. A hugely popular modern subgenre.

Re:Zero, Sword Art Online
