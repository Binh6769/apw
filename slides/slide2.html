What Is Anime?

Anime is hand-drawn and computer-generated animation originating from Japan. The word itself is the Japanese abbreviation for "animation" and refers to a distinctive style characterized by vibrant graphics, colorful characters, and fantastical themes.

Key insight: Unlike Western cartoons, anime spans all genres and demographics — from children's shows to adult dramas, horror, romance, and philosophical sci-fi.

Distinct Art

Expressive eyes, unique hair colors, exaggerated facial expressions, and detailed backgrounds.

Serialized Stories

Episodic arcs with complex plots, character development, and emotional depth across seasons.

Global Reach

Streaming platforms like Crunchyroll, Netflix, and Funimation bring anime to 200+ countries.
