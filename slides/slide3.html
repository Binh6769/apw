History of Anime

From early experimental films to a multi-billion dollar global industry — anime's journey spans over a century.

1917 - 1960

Early Beginnings

First Japanese animated films emerge. Influenced by Western animation but quickly develops its own identity.

  • First anime: Namakura Gatana (1917)
  • Disney influence on early style

1963 - 1980

Tezuka Era

Osamu Tezuka pioneers modern anime with Astro Boy. Big eyes, limited animation, and serialized TV format.

  • Astro Boy (1963) - first TV anime
  • Speed Racer, Lupin III

1980s - 1990s

Golden Age

Rise of OVAs, Studio Ghibli, and cyberpunk. Anime goes international with Akira and Dragon Ball Z.

  • Akira (1988), Ghost in the Shell
  • Dragon Ball Z, Sailor Moon

2000+

Modern Era

Digital animation, global streaming, and mainstream acceptance. Anime becomes a worldwide phenomenon.

  • Attack on Titan, Demon Slayer
  • Crunchyroll, Netflix simulcasts
