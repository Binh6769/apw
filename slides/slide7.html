Famous Anime Studios

These studios have shaped anime with their distinctive styles and legendary works.

Studio Ghibli

Hand-drawn beauty, environmental themes, and emotional depth. Founded by Hayao Miyazaki.

Spirited Away, My Neighbor Totoro

MAPPA

Modern powerhouse. Fluid animation and dark, mature adaptations. Rapidly rising since 2010s.

Jujutsu Kaisen, Chainsaw Man

Kyoto Animation

Pristine art, emotional depth, and exceptional character animation. Meticulous detail.

Violet Evergarden, K-On!

Ufotable

Masters of visual effects and seamless 2D/3D combat animation.

Demon Slayer, Fate/Zero
